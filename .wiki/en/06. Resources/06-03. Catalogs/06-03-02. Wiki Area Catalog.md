# Wiki Area Catalog

This catalog defines how PARA Areas are handled in the base wiki. Areas are long-running responsibilities, not temporary projects.

| Area | Responsibility | Recommended location |
|---|---|---|
| Wiki Operations | structure, templates, maps, catalogs, validation | `05. Areas` |
| Product Knowledge | private user or organization domain knowledge | user wiki |
| Project Knowledge | time-bound work and deliverables | `04. Projects` |
| Source Material | external documents and source-backed evidence | `02. Literature Notes` |
