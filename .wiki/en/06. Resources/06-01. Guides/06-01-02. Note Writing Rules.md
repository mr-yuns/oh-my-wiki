# Note Writing Rules

The base wiki uses PARA for placement and Zettelkasten-style links for relationships. Folders express operating state; links express meaning.

## Principles

- One note should cover one question, rule, source, or concept.
- Separate confirmed knowledge from observations and assumptions.
- Mark unverified material explicitly.
- Keep filenames and H1 titles aligned where possible.
- Link long-lived notes to at least one map, catalog, hub, or related note.
- Do not place organization-specific, customer-specific, or personal material in the public base wiki.

## Required Folders

```text
01. Inbox
02. Literature Notes
03. Permanent Notes
04. Projects
05. Areas
06. Resources
07. Archive
08. Templates
```
