# AI Tool Integration Principles

This guide keeps the base wiki independent of any single AI tool.

## Principles

- Treat AI output as a proposal until reviewed.
- Store tool names in capture context, not long-lived note titles.
- Mark unverified summaries and code interpretations clearly.
- Do not store organization-specific authentication or account flows in the public base wiki.
