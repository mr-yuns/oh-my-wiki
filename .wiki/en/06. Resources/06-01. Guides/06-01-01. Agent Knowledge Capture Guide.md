# Agent Knowledge Capture Guide

This guide defines when agent output should become reusable base wiki knowledge. Public base wiki content must stay free of organization-specific products, customers, credentials, private URLs, and personal notes.

## Capture Criteria

- Keep reusable rules, procedures, decision criteria, and templates.
- Do not keep team-private decisions, customer details, credentials, unpublished product details, or personal notes.
- Raw material may be temporary input, but public distribution must contain only generic examples.
- Promote only verified facts, constraints, exceptions, and validation steps.
