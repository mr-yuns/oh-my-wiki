# Wiki Operating Procedure

This procedure keeps the base wiki maintainable, reviewable, and safe to publish.

## Change Procedure

1. Confirm the change is reusable and safe for public base wiki distribution.
2. Create new notes from templates and fill required metadata.
3. Check numbering, links, tags, and parent hubs.
4. Generalize or remove organization-specific knowledge.
5. Keep runtime logic, scripts, generated indexes, and `.omw` managed state out of the tracked base wiki.
6. Run wiki validation and package dry-run before publishing.
